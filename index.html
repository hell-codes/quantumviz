Wave–Particle Duality

Quantum particles exhibit both particle-like and wave-like behaviour. The wavefunction ψ(x) describes the quantum state of the particle.

|ψ(x)|² → Probability Density
|ψ(x)|² — the probability density function.
Integrating over any region gives the probability of finding the particle there.

ψ(x) — the wavefunction (complex-valued)
|·|² — modulus squared (removes complex phase)
